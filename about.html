Policy moves fast. You shouldn't have to chase it.

Indian policy information is scattered across government portals, regulatory websites, court databases, and news publications. A single regulatory change might surface as a gazette notification, an RBI circular, and three news reports — none linking to each other. PolicyRadar brings it all together.

How it works

Every day, PolicyRadar aggregates policy-relevant content from 330+ sources — government press releases, regulatory circulars, court decisions, news coverage, think tank analysis — and classifies it across 22 policy sectors. Each article is tagged by source type and scored by priority, so you see what matters first.

Three views give you different angles: the Dashboard for today's feed and top stories, the Intelligence Hub for sector-level deep dives, and Connections for keyword exploration and signal monitoring — tracking which topics are emerging, accelerating, or going quiet.

Who it's for

Compliance teams, policy researchers, journalists covering regulation, legal professionals, think tank analysts, and anyone whose work depends on knowing what Indian regulators and lawmakers did today.
