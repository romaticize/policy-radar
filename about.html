About PolicyRadar

India's policy landscape moves fast — across hundreds of government departments, regulators, courts, and legislatures. PolicyRadar watches all of it so you don't have to.

Every day, we pull from 330+ sources — RBI, SEBI, PIB, state governments, news outlets, legal publications, think tanks — and surface the policy developments that matter. The site updates daily. The weekly newsletter adds editorial analysis: what happened, why it matters, and what to watch next.
