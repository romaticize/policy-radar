Policy moves fast. You shouldn't have to chase it.

PolicyRadar tracks Indian policy developments across 330+ sources — government press releases, regulatory circulars, court decisions, legislative updates, and news coverage — so you see the full picture in one place, every day.

The problem

Indian policy information is scattered across dozens of government portals, regulatory websites, and news publications. A single regulatory change might appear as a gazette notification on egazette.gov.in, a circular on rbi.org.in, a news report in the Economic Times, and an analysis in a think tank journal — all within 48 hours, none linking to each other.

Policy professionals, compliance teams, researchers, and journalists spend hours every morning checking multiple sources manually. Critical updates get missed. Context gets lost. Nobody has a complete view.

What PolicyRadar does

PolicyRadar aggregates policy-relevant content from across India's information landscape — government press releases, RBI and SEBI circulars, regulatory notifications, court decisions, news coverage, and think tank analysis — and organises it into a single, searchable daily feed.

Every article is classified by policy sector (22 categories spanning finance, technology, governance, energy, defence, trade, and more), tagged by source type (government, news, legal, research, industry), and scored by priority. The result is a daily intelligence feed where you can see not just what happened, but which sectors are heating up, which sources are covering what, and how stories are developing across multiple outlets.

Three ways to explore

Dashboard

The daily feed. Today's policy summary, top stories by category, trending topics with multi-source coverage, and a filterable article stream across every sector. Filter by policy domain, category, source type, priority, or time period.

Intelligence Hub

Sector-level deep dives. Pick a policy sector and see its source-type breakdown, coverage trends, and article-level exploration. Understand not just what's being covered, but who's covering it and how deeply.

Connections

Keyword explorer and signal monitor. Search any policy topic and see which sectors it spans, which sources cover it, and related keywords. Policy Pulse tracks which topics are newly emerging, accelerating, or going quiet — early warnings of where policy attention is shifting.

What we cover

PolicyRadar monitors 330+ sources across six categories:

  • Government — PIB press releases, RBI and SEBI circulars, TRAI updates, regulatory notifications
  • News — National and business media covering policy implications
  • Legal — Court decisions, legal analysis, regulatory orders
  • Research — Think tanks, policy research institutions, and analysis organisations
  • Industry — Trade associations, industry bodies, professional publications
  • International — Global coverage of India-relevant policy developments

Articles are processed daily. The platform currently indexes thousands of articles per week across all 22 policy categories.

Built for

PolicyRadar is designed for policy professionals, compliance teams at technology companies, researchers, journalists covering regulation and governance, legal professionals tracking regulatory developments, think tank analysts, and students studying Indian public policy.

If your work depends on knowing what the Indian government, regulators, and courts did today — and what it means — PolicyRadar is built for you.
