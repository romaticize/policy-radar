Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Rules
Rules Pending
Act passed: August 2023 · Rules expected: 2026
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Rules/Assent
The parent Act was passed in 2023 but enforcement depends on subordinate rules that MeitY has yet to notify. Draft rules were circulated in January 2025 for public consultation. Key provisions cover consent managers, Data Protection Board composition, cross-border data transfer conditions, and children's data safeguards.

Waqf (Amendment) Bill, 2024
In Committee
Introduced: August 2024 · Referred to Joint Parliamentary Committee
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Assent
Proposes sweeping changes to waqf property governance: mandatory Aadhaar-linked registration, central portal for all waqf properties, district collector authority over property disputes (replacing waqf tribunals), and revenue department survey powers. The JPC received over 70 lakh representations. Expected to return in Monsoon Session 2026.

Constitution (129th Amendment) Bill — One Nation One Election
In Committee
Introduced: December 2024 · Referred to Joint Parliamentary Committee
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Assent
Seeks to synchronize Lok Sabha and state assembly elections. Requires constitutional amendment (two-thirds majority in both houses) plus ratification by at least half the state legislatures. The Kovind Committee recommended simultaneous elections by 2029. Opposition calls it an assault on federalism.

Telecommunications Act, 2023 — Remaining Rules
Rules Pending
Act passed: December 2023 · Several rules still pending notification
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Rules/Assent
Replaces the Indian Telegraph Act (1885) and Indian Wireless Telegraphy Act (1933). Key pending rules cover: spectrum assignment and auction procedures, interception and monitoring safeguards, right of way for telecom infrastructure, and OTT regulation framework. Some provisions already notified; others awaited.

Coastal Shipping Bill, 2024
Introduced
Introduced: Rajya Sabha, December 2024
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Assent
Aims to promote coastal and inland shipping as an alternative freight mode. Proposes a national coastal shipping development fund, licensing framework for coastal trade, and cabotage relaxation measures. Part of the Sagarmala programme's next phase.

Banking Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2024
Passed Lok Sabha
Passed Lok Sabha: April 2025 · Rajya Sabha pending
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Assent
Amends the Banking Regulation Act, RBI Act, and SBI Act to allow up to four nominees per bank account (from one), align regulatory definitions, and strengthen RBI's supervisory powers over cooperative banks. A relatively technical but consumer-impactful reform.

Boilers Bill, 2024
Passed Both Houses
Passed: Both Houses, 2025 · Awaiting Presidential Assent
Introduced Committee Lok Sabha Rajya Sabha Assent
Replaces the 1923 Indian Boilers Act. Modernizes industrial safety standards for boiler manufacturing and operation, introduces third-party inspections, and decriminalizes minor procedural violations while strengthening penalties for safety failures.
