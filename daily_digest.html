Contractor Entitled to GST Reimbursement Under Contract Clause Despite Missing Schedule A: Tripura HC
Tripura and Bombay High Courts ruled in favor of taxpayers in separate GST cases: the Tripura HC allowed a contractor’s GST reimbursement under a contract clause despite a missing Schedule A, while the Bombay HC set aside a refund rejection for violating Rule 92(3) procedure. Additionally, the Bombay HC held employees not liable for GST penalties due to lack of taxable person status, and the Andhra Pradesh HC ruled that an SGST officer lacked jurisdiction over IGST on imports, as only customs authorities could assess such taxes.
