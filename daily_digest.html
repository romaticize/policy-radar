RBI MPC begins policy meet amid West Asia crisis; decision on Wednesday
The Reserve Bank of India's (RBI) Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) has begun its policy meeting amid the West Asia crisis, with a decision expected on Wednesday. RBI Governor has cautioned that the West Asia conflict could impede growth and increase imported inflation, while the central bank is also addressing concerns over a weak rupee. The RBI has previously cut rates by a total of 125 basis points since February 2025.

RBI invites public comments on the draft Amendment Directions on review of methodology for identification of NBFC-UL and inclusion of Government owned NBFCs in NBFC-UL
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has invited public comments on draft Amendment Directions regarding the review of the methodology for identifying NBFC-UL (Upper Layer Non-Banking Financial Companies). The draft proposes defining upper-layer NBFCs based on an absolute asset size of ₹1 lakh crore or more and includes government-owned NBFCs, including PSUs, in this category.
