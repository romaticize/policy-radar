RBI Issues Revised Draft Directions on Loan Recovery & Recovery Agents
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has issued revised draft directions on loan recovery and recovery agents, proposing stricter borrower protection norms effective from October 1. The draft rules also include provisions allowing lenders to disable smartphone functions in case of default. Stakeholders, including entities like Amendment Directions, Capital Adequacy, Issues Revised Draft Directions, Loan Recovery, and Recovery Agents, have been invited to provide feedback on these proposals.
