RBI proposes revised capital adequacy disclosure norms for banks
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has proposed revised capital adequacy disclosure norms for banks under Basel Pillar 3, requiring detailed information on capital and risks. Additionally, the RBI has introduced stricter borrower protection norms for loan recovery, effective from October 1, including bans on abusive language and blocking mobile phones by recovery agents and bank employees.
