RBI Invites Comments on Draft Directions for NBFC-UL Identification & Govt NBFC Inclusion
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has invited public comments on draft directions regarding the identification of Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) as systemically important (NBFC-UL) and the inclusion of government-owned NBFCs in this category. Additionally, the RBI has issued a master direction amending regulations on non-resident investment in debt instruments.

RBI issues Discussion Paper on Exploring safeguards in digital payments to curb frauds
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has issued a Discussion Paper proposing safeguards to curb digital payment frauds, including potential 1-hour delays and customer controls. The paper, titled Digital Payment Fraud Safeguards, is open for public comments and aims to enhance security in cross-border inward payments, as outlined in the Faster Cross initiative. The RBI also seeks feedback on measures for Timely Credit and Border Inward Payments to address rising fraud cases.
