Silver rate today: MCX silver price falls 4% to ₹2.79 lk/kg on firm dollar, crude price rise; Trump-Xi meeting awaited
Silver prices in India fell to ₹2.79 lakh per kg, down 4%, amid a stronger dollar and rising crude oil prices, while gold also declined by ₹1,600 due to inflation concerns linked to tensions involving Iran. Investors are closely monitoring the upcoming Trump-Xi meeting, as geopolitical risks, including Iran-related threats to oil supply routes, continue to influence commodity markets. Crude oil prices rose over 1% following warnings about Iran and concerns over the Strait of Hormuz.
