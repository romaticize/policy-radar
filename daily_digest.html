MPC members voted for status quo on rates amid W. Asia crisis: RBI minutes
The Reserve Bank of India’s Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) maintained the current interest rates, citing concerns over inflation risks linked to energy prices, the West Asia conflict, and potential El Niño effects.
