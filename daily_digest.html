Netherlands returns Chola-era copper plates to India during PM Modi’s visit
The Netherlands returned the 11th-century Century Chola Copper Plates to India during Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s visit, marking the culmination of a 14-year effort. The repatriation was announced by multiple sources, including IE India, ThePrint Diplomacy, and Deccan Chronicle, highlighting its significance as a historic homecoming. The event coincided with Modi’s meeting with the Dutch King, as reported by CNBC Latest.

Tata Electronics and ASML partner on India’s first semiconductor fab
Tata Electronics and ASML have partnered to establish India’s first advanced semiconductor fabrication facility in Gujarat. The collaboration aims to boost domestic semiconductor manufacturing capabilities in India. The agreement was reported by multiple sources, including IE Technology, HT India, Hindu BusinessLine Tech, and ET Policy.

Andhra Pradesh to give Rs 40,000 for fourth child, Rs 30,000 for third: CM Chandrababu Naidu
The Andhra Pradesh government, led by Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu, will provide cash incentives of ₹30,000 for a third child and ₹40,000 for a fourth child. The policy aims to address concerns about an ageing population in the state. Multiple sources confirm these details.

UAE to complete second oil pipeline bypassing strait of Hormuz by 2027
India, Israel, and Lebanon have extended a ceasefire for 45 days following talks in Washington, while the UAE is advancing plans to complete a second oil pipeline bypassing the Strait of Hormuz by 2027. The UAE aims to double its oil exports by 2027 through this pipeline, reducing reliance on the Strait of Hormuz. Additionally, India has raised concerns at the UN about the targeting of vessels in the Strait of Hormuz.
