Prepared to bear consequences: Arvind Kejriwal to boycott Excise policy case proceedings before Justice Swarana Kanta Sharma
Arvind Kejriwal announced he would boycott proceedings in the liquor policy case before Justice Swarana Kanta Sharma, stating he would not appear in person or through a lawyer. Congress Leader Pawan Khera Moves Supreme Court Against Gauhati HC's Rejection Of Anticipatory Bail In FIR By Assam CM's Wife
Congress leader Pawan Khera has moved the Supreme Court against the Gauhati High Court's rejection of his anticipatory bail plea in an FIR lodged by Assam Chief Minister's wife. The FIR pertains to allegations made against the Assam CM's wife, and the Gauhati High Court had denied anticipatory bail, stating it was not a simpliciter defamation case. The Supreme Court challenge follows the High Court's decision to reject his bail plea.
