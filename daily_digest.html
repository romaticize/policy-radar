Delhi HC Quashes GST Bank Account Attachment as Petitioner Furnished ₹1.5 Crore Security
Multiple High Courts have quashed or upheld GST-related actions based on procedural grounds. The Delhi HC set aside a bank account attachment after the petitioner furnished ₹1.5 crore security, while the Madras HC ruled that registration suspension without a proper show-cause notice (SCN) was unwarranted. Additionally, the Rajasthan HC upheld GST appeals, stating the 90-day limit under Section 101(2) is directory, and the HP HC invalidated an SCN relying on an unchallenged advance ruling.
